Security & Privacy Transparency

At Space Translation, your privacy and system security are our absolute priority. Because our application interacts with your clipboard and keyboard to provide a seamless "inject" experience, we want to be 100% transparent about how it works under the hood.

0/70 Detections on VirusTotal (Clean)

🛡️ 0% Tracking Policy

Space Translation is designed to be a local tool. We do not track, store, or monitor what you translate. There are no analytics trackers, no crash reporters sending your data to our servers, and no telemetry. The only internet connection established is directly between your computer and the translation engines (Google, Bing, Lingva, MyMemory).

⌨️ Why Keyboard Hooks? (Is it a Keylogger?)

Absolutely NOT. Space Translation is not a keylogger.

To provide the "magical" experience of translating and injecting text without switching windows, the application listens for specific global shortcuts (like Ctrl + Home or Ctrl + End). When triggered, it copies your highlighted text or types the translated text back into your active window (using native tools like `pyautogui` on Windows or `xdotool` on Linux).

The code only listens for those specific shortcut combinations. It does not record your keystrokes, and it does not save your clipboard history.

🔒 Secure Connections

Our application establishes direct and secure connections exclusively with the official translation engines. This ensures your data cannot be intercepted by Man-in-The-Middle (MITM) attacks while maintaining maximum speed and privacy.
